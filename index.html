Mini-Wiki is a small educational knowledge website that presents clear and concise explanations of selected science-fiction concepts. The site is designed to organize information in a simple and easy-to-read structure.

About Mini-Wiki

Mini-Wiki provides brief articles on topics such as alien life, time travel, simulation theory, parallel universes, and the technological singularity. Each article focuses on key ideas, definitions, and simplified explanations to make complex concepts easier to understand.

Topics Covered
  • Alien Life (Extraterrestrial existence)
  • Time Travel and temporal paradoxes
  • Simulation Theory
  • Parallel Universes (Multiverse concept)
  • Technological Singularity and human–technology merging
How to Use This Site

Use the navigation menu at the top of the page to explore different topics. Each page contains a short explanation and organized information to help readers quickly understand the concept.

This website is created as part of a web design assignment to demonstrate the use of structured HTML, semantic elements, and basic page organization.
